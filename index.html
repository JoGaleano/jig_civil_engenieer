JOSE IGNACIO GOMEZ | CIVIL ENGINEER

We live in a globalized world in which technology became part of our lives. However, the construction industry seems reluctant to take the step towards digital transformation.

The world saw the rise of the automotive and telecommunications industries in the XIX century; Nowadays these industries shapeshift our world by using cutting-edge technology. The telegraph evolved into cellphones, the steam locomotive was left behind giving way to autonomous vehicles, while in construction we continue using an invention from 5,000 years ago: fired brick.

The current power of computers makes them a very useful tool for engineers, since they allow extensive calculations and tedious mathematics to be solved, giving the professional more time for engineering, design and development.

I'm part of the group of professionals who want to actively contribute to the digital transformation of construction and bring industry 4.0.
